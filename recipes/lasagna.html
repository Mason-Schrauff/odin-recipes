Lasagna

Description

Easy to make Lasagna Recipe using a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper15 oz.

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Noodles and Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions.
  3. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Add garlic during the last minute.
  4. Add the chicken broth. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°.
  6. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for noodles.
  7. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce in casserole dish and add 4 lasagna noodles.
  8. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 ½ cups of meat sauce.
  9. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1 ½ cups meat sauce.
  10. Finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  11. Top with 2 ½ cups of Mozzarella cheese.
  12. Bake for 35 minutes.
  13. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it sit for 15 minutes prior to serving.
